Minimize the following function:

$$f(x, y) = 20 + x^2 + y^2  - 10 \cos(\frac{\pi}{2}x) - 10 \cos(\frac{\pi}{2}y)$$

Initialize within $$[-4, 4]^2$$. Comment on the convergence results for various
initial points.
